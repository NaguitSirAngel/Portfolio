Capstone


MAGITS: Martial Arts Gym Information Tracking System

A system for Martial Arts Gyms that will help Administrators and staffs to track member's attendance. It also allows staff to post announcements and courses that they offer and in turn, members are able to enroll and withdraw from them.






The system has 3 kinds of users (Admin, Instructors and Members), each member has their own view once logged in to the system. The system was designed and developed to identify the user's credentials to provide the right view.



Administrator View

The Administrator is the ultimate User in the system. Thus being able to do all basic CRUD operations from adding an Instructor to the system to posting announcements for the Members. The Administrator is able to add Courses that are offered in their Martial Arts Gym and dictate the pricing and typing of the membership (session based or monthly subscription). The Administrator is also able to view sessions and in those sessions data will be available for viewing. This will give them a better business perspective and insight that will be truly beneficial for the Marital Arts Gym and to its future business procedures.






Instructor View

The Instructor is the "Pseudo-Admin" User in the system. Being able to do actions such as adding Members to the system, posting announcements and activating sessions for the Members to attend. Once a sessions is activated, Members are able to attend the session by letting a Gym Staff (or Instructor in some cases) scan their ID QR Code that is provided by the system as well, doing so allows the recording of attendance data.






Member View

Members are the "Clients" of the Martial Arts Gym. As Members is the system, they are able to view the courses they currently are enrolled in. Members are also able to view the course's information such as the schedule, description and the Instructor that is going to teach. The Member view has a section that shows their ID QR Code that will be used for attendance. The Members are also able to enroll and withdraw from courses. Furthermore the Members are also able to view the sessions that they have attended and leave a comment or feedback for the Administrator and Instructor to see.
